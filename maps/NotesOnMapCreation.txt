Characters Used:
  Uppercase oh ("O") = Open Space
  Uppercase ex ("X") = Blocked Space
  Uppercase ess ("S") = Start Position
  Uppercase ef ("F") = Ending Position
  
Map Layout:
  Upper Left Corner is Origin (0,0)
  Lower Left Corner is (Max, Max)
  All Characters are delimited by spaces
  
Example:

S O X 
X O X
X O F

S is at 0,0, F is at 2,2
